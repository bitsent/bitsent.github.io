LINKs are encrypted files uploaded to the Bitcoin SV blockchain, which are easily accessible with a simple click.

The purpose of LINK is File Sharing.
Share immutable information with anyone, anywhere, by simply sending them a LINK to click on.
Nobody else should be able to see your data.
